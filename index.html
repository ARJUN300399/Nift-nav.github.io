NIFT BENGALURU CAMPUS TOUR

NIFT Bengaluru campus has a very dynamic topography and overall it makes a circular shape encompassing all the major sections. Its very well ventilated and blends in with the locality easily.

HAPPENING SPOTS

Amphitheatre

Heart of the campus which hosts a wide spectrum of activities. Be it apmhi-cricket, open mics or cultural fests. Have a seat and you'll never feel leaving.

Atrium

A place where you take an edge off. It the junction point connecting all parts of the campus.

Gallery

This beautiful path leads you to different parts of the campus. You'll encounter walls covered in graffitis and artworks that'll make you stop and adore.

Oval

The part of the campus which is very dynamic. Some days it's the hotspot for all the gossips, other times it's a go to place for doing your assignments. You might find people jamming there and dancing.
